Yixuan Wang 汪怡暄

I am a MS/PhD student in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. Before entering UIUC. I was an undergraduate student in the University of Michigan, major in Computer Science. I also obtained a B.S.E. in Mechanical Engineering in Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I am working on the integration of perception and manipulation under the supervision of Prof. Kris Hauser. I worked on deformable object tracking under the supervision of Dr. Dale McConachie in the Autonomous Robotic Manipulation Lab, investigated by Dr. Dmitry Berenson. I also worked on soft robot controlling based on perception information under the supervision of Dr. Audrey Sedal in the Compliant Systems Design Laboratory, investigated by Dr. Sridhar Kota.
